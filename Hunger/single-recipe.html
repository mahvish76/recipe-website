Simple sushi with your desired Ingredients

29 Oct, 2019 43 views 4.1
By Hina Khan
Breakfast
  • Prep: 1 hr
  • Time: 20 min
  • Yield: 20 roles

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoon rice vineger
  • 3 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled, cus into small strips
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger
  • 1 avicado
  • 1/2 pound limitation crabmeat, flaked

Sushi is easy and fun to make at home, and you can add your desired Ingredients onto the rice.
